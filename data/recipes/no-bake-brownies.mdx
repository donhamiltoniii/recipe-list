### Ingredients

#### Brownies

- 1 ¼ cup Roasted Almonds or raw almonds
- ⅓ cup Coconut Oil
- 1 cup Unsweetened Desiccated Coconut
- ½ cup + 1 tablespoon Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
- 1 tablespoon Coconut Flour
- ¼ cup Water
- ¼ cup Sugar-Free Maple Syrup

#### Frosting

- ⅓ cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
- ¼ cup Sugar-Free Maple Syrup
- 3 tablespoons Coconut Oil melted
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract

### Instructions

#### Brownies

1. Add all brownie ingredients to food processor.
1. Process on medium speed for 2-3 minutes until ingredients are incorporated.
1. Transfer batter 8x8 pan lined with parchment paper.
1. Freeze the brownie while you prepare the frosting.

#### Frosting

1. Double boil coconut oil and sweetener
1. Add in vanilla and cocoa until well incorporated and frost brownies
1. Return to freezer until frosting is set
1. With a warm knife, cut into 16 brownies
