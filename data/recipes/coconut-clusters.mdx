### Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups coconut chips or desiccated, unsweetened
- ¼ cup chia seeds
- ¼ cup hemp seeds
- ½ cup pumpkin seeds
- 2 tbsp liquid monkfruit sweetener or other liquid sweetener

### Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, place all the ingredients and mix well.
1. Spoon walnut sized shapes of the mixture together and slightly squeeze to hold a shape. Place the clusters on a parchment paper lined tray.
1. Bake at 350F degrees for 15 minutes.
1. Allow to cool before removing the clusters from the tray. Place in the fridge to harden as they can break.
