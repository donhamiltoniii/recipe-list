### Ingredients

- 2 medium sweet potatoes, diced into 1/2-inch cubes (about 2 cups, 300g)
- 1 lb (450g) ground turkey
- 1 can (15 oz/425g) black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 bell pepper, diced (about 150g)
- 4 cups fresh spinach (120g)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil (30ml)
- 2 teaspoons taco seasoning
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 avocado, sliced (optional, 150g)

**Simple Dressing:**

- 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt (60g)
- 1 tablespoon lime juice (15ml)

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Toss sweet potatoes with 1 tablespoon olive oil, salt, and pepper.
3. Roast for 20-25 minutes until tender.
4. Meanwhile, heat remaining oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Add ground turkey, breaking it up with a spoon. Cook until no longer pink, about 7-8 minutes.
6. Add bell pepper and taco seasoning. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
7. Stir in black beans, cook until heated through.
8. Add spinach and stir until just wilted, about 1-2 minutes.
9. Mix yogurt and lime juice in a small bowl.
10. Serve sweet potatoes topped with turkey-bean-greens mixture, yogurt dressing, and avocado if using.
