### Ingredients

#### Casserole

- 8 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
- 1/4 cup mayonnaise
- 2 cups shredded cheese of choice
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 2 tsp ground black pepper
- 3 tsp salt
- 3 cups pulled chicken (you can also get a rotisserie chicken and break that down for faster prep)
- 3 cups broccoli

#### Topping

- 2 cups shredded cheese of choice

### Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
1. Cut chicken into 1 inch chunks and saute (skip if using rotisserie)
1. Steam broccoli
1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and stir until well combined
1. Transfer mixture to a 9x13 baking dish and sprinkle with the remaining cheese.
1. Bake for 15 minutes or until top is golden brown.
