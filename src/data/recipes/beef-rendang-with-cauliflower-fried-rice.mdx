### Ingredients

#### Fried Rice

- 2 large head cauliflower, riced
- 1 cup frozen peas
- 1 cup diced carrots
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

#### Beef Rendang

- 500g lean beef, cut into chunks
- 1 can of coconut milk
- 2 tbsp rendang curry paste
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt to taste

### Instructions

#### Fried Rice

1. Heat two tbsp sesame oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add the riced cauliflower, peas, and carrots.
3. Stir-fry for about 10 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
4. Season with salt and crushed pepper and serve warm.

#### Beef Rendang

1. Heat one tbsp oil in a large stockpot over medium heat. Add curry paste and saut√© until fragrant.
2. Add beef pieces and cook until brown on all sides.
3. Add coconut milk and take it to a simmer. Decrease the stove heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 2 hours or until the beef is tender and the sauce has thickened.
4. Season with salt to taste before serving.
