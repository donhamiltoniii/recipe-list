### Ingredients

- 1 bag spinach (~10oz)
- 1 can of beans (whatever you have or want)
- 2oz cheese (feta or shredded parmesan or goat cheese)
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1/8 cup ACV

### Instructions

1. Put everything in a bowl and shake it up or mix it with your hands or whatever you want to do to evenly distribute all ingredients
