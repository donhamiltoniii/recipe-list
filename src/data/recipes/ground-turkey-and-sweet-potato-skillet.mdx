### Ingredients

- Ground turkey (1 pound)
- Sweet potatoes (2 large, peeled and diced)
- Black beans (1 can, about 15 ounces, drained and rinsed)
- Cheese (1 cup shredded, such as cheddar or Monterey Jack)
- Taco seasoning (1 packet, or 2-3 tablespoons of homemade blend)

### Instructions

1. Peel and saute sweet potatoes.
2. Once sweet potatoes are soft, add turkey and brown.
3. Once turkey is browned, add remaining ingredients and season.
4. Serve. Hot. And be hot serving it.
