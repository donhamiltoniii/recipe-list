### Ingredients

- 1 (16-ounce) jar mild or medium salsa
- Â¼ cup Dijon mustard
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts

### Instructions

1 In a small bowl, combine salsa, mustard, and lime juice. Stir well.
2 Place chicken in a 4- to 6-quart slow cooker and cover with salsa mixture.
3 Cover and cook on high for 4 hours or on low for 6 hours or until chicken is cooked all the way through.
4 Serve chicken with sauce from slow cooker.
