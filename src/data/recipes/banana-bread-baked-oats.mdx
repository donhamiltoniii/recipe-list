### Ingredients

- 4 large eggs
- 4 very ripe bananas
- 3 cup old fashioned rolled oats
- 3 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 3 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 cup chocolate chunks

### Instructions

1. Add eggs, bananas (save about half of a banana for slicing on top), oats, vanilla extract, salt, cinnamon, and baking powder to a blender. Blend on high until a creamy batter forms, about 30 seconds.
1. Slice up the remaining half a banana into coins.
1. Pour batter into air fryer
1. Add chocolate chips if using and stir.
1. Add some chocolate chips and banana slices on top if preferred.
1. Pop them in your air fryer and cook at 300 degrees F for 10-15 minutes. Oats will be done when a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
