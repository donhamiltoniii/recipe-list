### Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 1‚ÅÑ2 cup olive oil mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp white vinegar
- 1 tbsp horseradish mustard
- S & P to taste
- cayenne

### Instructions

1. Peel eggs and cut in half lengthwise. Scoop out egg yolks and put in a small mixing bowl.
2. Combine yolks, mayonnaise, vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper. A mixer really helps.
3. Put equal amounts of mixture in each halved egg. Sprinkle with cayenne.
